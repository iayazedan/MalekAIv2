Our Story

Crafted by Kid

Built for Kids, Inspired by Malek

Malek AI began with a simple but powerful question from a curious 6-year-old named Malek. One day, while using another generative AI to help with his homework, Malek asked a question beyond his years: ‘How did I come into this life?’ The AI, designed for general users, provided answers that were far too mature for a child to understand. Malek, a little puzzled and concerned, turned to us and asked, ‘Why don’t we have something like this, but made just for kids?

Malek AI was born from a simple question asked by a curious 6-year-old, sparking the vision for an AI platform built just for kids
